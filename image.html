Introduction about our Typing Master Software.

Typing Master Software is a computer software program designed to enhance a person's typing ability in terms of speed and accuracy. It is also used to teach correct the positioning of fingers on the keyboard for better posture and ease of typing. It demonstrates how to position the hands over the keys and how to type quickly to improve your speed. The software provides customizable and adaptable training to suit the individual needs of the user. Typing Master credits its success on a user-friendly design that makes it fast and easy to learn typing techniques through graduated challenges, interactive game and comprehensive progress reports, which pinpoint weak spots. The program customizes exercises depending on the needs of the user, and is fully adaptable. Typing is both physically and mentally exhausting to do for a long period of time. Typing Master will increase both physical and mental capabilities to stay typing for a long period of time.
